Red Lorry Film Festival 2026

About the movie


From Oscar contenders and iconic classics to bold cinema from Korea, China, France, Germany, and beyond, plus treasured Marathi masterpieces, masterclasses, star led events, and red carpet premieres, Red Lorry Film Festival becomes Mumbai's biggest gateway to world cinema this year. The festival will take place in Mumbai from 13th to 15th March 2026.

Building on the success of its first two editions, the Red Lorry Film Festival is back with its third edition, showcasing 120+ carefully curated films with unlimited access across multiple categories, spanning 15+ languages from across the world, in a bigger, bolder avatar as '3.0' in Mumbai.

This pass gives you access to all the participating cinemas including the Red Lorry Fest Hub - Maison Inox, Jio World Plaza.

This time around, get ready to be enthralled by premiere screenings and movies from across the globe and sneak previews.

Hurry! Limited passes available due to limited venues.

So, hop onboard!
